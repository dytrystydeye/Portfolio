Hello, I'm Kyle

I am a graphic designer based in Vancouver, Canada. I graduated from the BCIT Graphic Arts program in 2024, where I earned my certificate and now moving towards New media and Design My passion for design is especially focused on Adobe Illustrator and UI/UX, as I find joy in creating icons, wire frames and developing userflow. I am eager to pursue a career that allows me to specialize in these areas. My creative process involves thorough planning and active collaboration with others. I believe that engaging with different views and carefully considering each design challenge leads to more answers. By doing this way this not only improve my work but also keeps me motivated. In the future, my goal is to secure a position as a junior UI/UX designer that is stable and give me chances for growth. I aim to contribute to projects that are not only impactful but also memorable, leaving a lasting impression through thoughtful and well-crafted designs.
